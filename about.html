About Us

Winnie Haizel Ministries

Winnie Haizel Ministries International Inc. is a Spirit-led global prayer ministry dedicated to transforming lives

Founded by Prophetess Winifred Haizel, the ministry serves as a sacred altar of prayer where individuals experience healing, deliverance, and deep spiritual renewal.

The ministry offers both in-person and virtual services, designed to lead people into a deeper relationship with God, empower daily spiritual growth, and foster a lifestyle of holiness and intimacy with the Holy Spirit.

Winnie Haizel Ministries is also home to Revival Tabernacle Ministry, a vibrant church community in Maryland where lives are continually impacted through powerful worship, prophetic preaching, and fervent prayer.

At its core, the ministry is committed to:

Winning souls for Christ.
Teaching the uncompromised Word of God.
Raising a generation anchored in prayer and spiritual discipline.
Engaging in community outreach to bring hope and transformation.

Meet Our Founder & Senior Pastor

Prophetess Winifred Haizel

Prophetess Winifred Haizel is a passionate intercessor, revivalist, and dynamic prophetic voice with a strong healing anointing and apostolic authority. Known for her deep commitment to prayer and the Word of God, she has devoted her life to spiritual restoration, soul-winning, and advancing God's Kingdom globally.

She is the author of two powerful books:

Let Love Lead
A guide to emotional healing and walking in forgiveness.
Miracles in Our Midst
A collection of testimonies revealing God's supernatural power through prayer.
View Books

Beyond ministry, Prophetess Haizel is the CEO of Haizels Events and Catering Services, a thriving business that reflects her passion for excellence. She is also the founder of the Rescue the Perishing Foundation and the visionary behind the Christ for the Hoods evangelistic campaign—initiatives designed to bring hope and revival to underserved communities.

Above all, she is a devoted mother of two, gracefully balancing family, ministry, and business while continuing to impact lives across the globe through prayer, healing, and spiritual transformation.
